Our Story

A journey of flavors from Hyderabad to your heart, preserving tradition while embracing innovation

From Heritage to Your Home

In the bustling streets of old Hyderabad, where the aroma of spices danced through narrow alleys, our story began. Aroma isn't just a cloud kitchen ‚Äì it's a bridge between centuries-old culinary traditions and modern convenience.

Every grain of rice, every blend of spice, carries with it the legacy of the Nizams and the passion of generations who perfected the art of biryani. Today, we bring this authentic taste directly to your doorstep, ensuring that distance never dilutes tradition.

Our commitment goes beyond just food delivery. We're preserving a cultural heritage, one biryani at a time, making sure that the authentic flavors of Hyderabad remain accessible to food lovers everywhere.

Our Journey Through Time

1950s

The Foundation

Our founder's established the original recipe in the heart of Hyderabad's old city, serving authentic dum biryani to local families and earning a reputation for uncompromising quality.

1980s

Perfecting the Art

The second generation refined the cooking techniques, introducing the signature 24-hour marination process and perfecting the balance of spices that makes our biryani truly exceptional.

2010

Expanding Horizons

Chef Rama brought the family recipes to Kenya, adapting traditional methods to local ingredients while maintaining the authentic taste that defines true Hyderabadi biryani.

2023

Aroma is Born

Launching as a premium cloud kitchen, Aroma combines traditional cooking methods with modern delivery systems, making authentic Hyderabadi biryani accessible to food lovers across the city.

Today

Your Trusted Partner

With hundreds of satisfied customers and a growing reputation for excellence, Aroma continues to serve authentic biryani while building lasting relationships with food enthusiasts who appreciate genuine flavors.
