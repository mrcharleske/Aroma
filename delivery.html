Delivery FAQ

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Last orders are accepted at 9:30 PM to ensure delivery before closing time.

How do I track my order?

Once you place an order via WhatsApp, we'll send you regular updates including preparation start, cooking completion, and dispatch notifications.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, M-Pesa, and bank transfers. Payment details will be shared when you place your order via WhatsApp.

Do you deliver during bad weather?

Yes, we deliver in most weather conditions. During severe weather, delivery times may be slightly extended for safety reasons.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Order modifications are possible within 10 minutes of placing the order, before preparation begins. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp.

What if I'm not satisfied with my delivery?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If there's any issue with your order, contact us immediately and we'll make it right.
